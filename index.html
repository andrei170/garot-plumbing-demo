Common Questions

Things Green Bay homeowners ask before they call.

Are you a 24/7 emergency plumber in Green Bay?
Yes. After-hours emergency calls (burst pipes, no hot water, sewer backup) are handled by an on-call Garot technician. Standard hours are Monday through Friday, 7:30 AM to 5:00 PM. Outside those hours, call 920-499-2202 and the line will route to the on-call technician.
How long has Garot Plumbing been in business?
Edwin C. Garot Co. Inc. has served Green Bay and the surrounding communities since 1946. We are now in our third generation of family ownership, making this our 80th year of plumbing in Northeast Wisconsin.
What areas do you serve?
Green Bay, De Pere, Allouez, Bellevue, Howard, Suamico, Ashwaubenon, and the surrounding Brown County communities. If your address falls inside Brown County, we cover you.
Do you replace water heaters the same day?
In most cases, yes. We stock the most common gas and electric water heater sizes (40-gallon, 50-gallon) on our trucks. If your water heater fails before noon, you usually have hot water back the same day.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. Garot Plumbing & Heating is fully licensed by the Wisconsin Department of Safety and Professional Services and carries full general liability and workers' compensation insurance. Proof of license and insurance is available on request.
Do you give estimates over the phone?
For a few standard items (water heater swap, sump pump replacement, basic drain clearing) we can give a price band over the phone. For everything else, we send a technician for a free in-home assessment, then quote a fixed price before any work begins.
