Common Questions

Things Green Bay homeowners ask before they call.

If your question isn't here, the dispatcher has answered every variation a thousand times. Just call.

How long has Garot Plumbing been in business?
Edwin C. Garot Co. Inc. has served Green Bay and the surrounding Brown County communities since 1946. That makes 2026 our 80th year of plumbing in Northeast Wisconsin, all under family ownership.
What areas do you serve?
Green Bay, De Pere, Allouez, Bellevue, Howard, Suamico, Ashwaubenon, Pulaski, Denmark, Luxemburg, Seymour, Oneida, Abrams, Krakow, Little Suamico, Mill Center, and New Franken. If your address falls inside Brown County or the surrounding communities, give us a call.
Do you replace water heaters the same day?
In most cases, yes. We stock the most common gas and electric water heater sizes (40-gallon, 50-gallon) on our trucks. If your water heater fails before noon, you usually have hot water back the same day.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. Garot Plumbing & Heating is fully licensed by the Wisconsin Department of Safety and Professional Services and carries full general liability and workers' compensation insurance. Proof of license and insurance is available on request.
Do you give estimates over the phone?
For a few standard items (water heater swap, sump pump replacement, basic drain clearing) we can give a price band over the phone. For everything else, we send a technician for an in-home assessment, then quote a fixed price before any work begins.
What payment methods do you accept?
Cash, check, Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. Payment is due on completion unless other arrangements have been made in advance.
